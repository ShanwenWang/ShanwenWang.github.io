Lean Project

My long term project is to formalize the results in $p$-adic Hodge theory, in particular the work of Jean-Marc Fontaine, Jean Pierre Wintenberger , Pierre Colmez, Christophe Breuil and Laurent Berger. At the moment, it is a quite ambitious project, but I hope that the development of technology will help me to achieve this goal.
